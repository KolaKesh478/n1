Campus Locations and Highlights

North Campus

205 Humber College Blvd., Toronto, Ontario, Canada M9W 5L7

Humber's largest campus is centrally located with easy access to the downtown core. Its balance of urban living and lush forestry makes this campus an ideal blend for all of our students.

North Campus

Lakeshore Campus

2 Colonel Samuel Smith Park Drive, Toronto, Ontario, Canada M8V 4B6

Located just west of Toronto on the shore of Lake Ontario, the Lakeshore campus offers beautiful historic architecture, modern conveniences and lots of ways to connect with nature.
